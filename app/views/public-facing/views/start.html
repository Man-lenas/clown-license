Use this service to apply, renew, replace or update your Clownery License and pay for it online.

The online service is also available in Welsh (ar gael yn Gymraeg).

You cannot apply with this service if you have already joined the Ministry of Silly Walks.

How long it takes

Registering takes 5 minutes.

Cost of service

The service costs 20 Smackeroonies (SKR).

Before you start

You will need the following details:

  • A portrait picture in full clown makeup.
  • An up-to-date list of skills and mad tricks.
  • Funemployment history.
  • Evidence of a Level 5 Clownery Qualification.

You can also register by post.
